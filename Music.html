Famous Music in Egypt

Umm Kulthum

Umm Kulthum, known as "The Star of the East," was an iconic Egyptian singer, songwriter, and film actress. Her powerful voice and emotional delivery have made her one of the most famous and beloved musicians in the Arab world.

Mohamed Abdel Wahab

Mohamed Abdel Wahab was a prominent 20th-century Egyptian singer and composer. He contributed to the development of modern Egyptian and Arabic music and composed many songs for Umm Kulthum.

Shaabi Music

Shaabi music is a popular genre of folk music in Egypt, characterized by its lively beats, catchy melodies, and lyrics that often reflect the lives and struggles of the working class. It has evolved over the years and remains a staple in Egyptian music culture.

Contemporary Egyptian Pop

Contemporary Egyptian pop music has gained widespread popularity, blending traditional Egyptian sounds with modern pop influences. Artists like Amr Diab, Tamer Hosny, and Sherine Abdel Wahab are some of the leading figures in this genre.
